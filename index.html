About me

Research-led. Evidence-based. Genuinely curious.

Senior Product Designer with 10+ years shaping complex SaaS and enterprise products. I specialise in research-led, end-to-end design for workflow-driven professional tools — turning ambiguity into clarity and translating user complexity into measurable outcomes.

I've spent most of my career designing for professionals in regulated industries — tax, legal, compliance — where accuracy, trust, and efficiency aren't nice-to-haves. I'm deeply collaborative, comfortable with ambiguity, and genuinely invested in elevating design maturity wherever I work.

Outside of design, I'm a certified Level 1 Nutrition Coach and Fitness trainer — applying the same user-centric empathy to health as I do to product.
